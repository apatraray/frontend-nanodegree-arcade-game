Arcade Game Instructions

Rules of Game:

  1. Player goal is to reach water without colliding enemies.
  2. On reaching water, player is rewarded with 10 points.
  3. On getting collided by enemy, there is deduction of 5 points.
  4. The player can move left, right, up and down.
  5. If the score is equal to or more than 100 points, the player is declared as winner
